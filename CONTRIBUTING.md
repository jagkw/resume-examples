You may contribute by:

- Correcting typos and minor content issues  
- Adding verified **future-skills resume examples** (AI, data, cybersecurity, product, automation, etc.)

> **All contributions are reviewed for quality, ATS-compatibility, and relevance.**

---

## ðŸš« What We Do *Not* Accept

To protect quality and user trust, we **do not accept**:

- Resume templates copied from competitors
- Low-quality or auto-generated AI dumps without proofreading
- Branded resume samples intended for promotion
- Resumes that are not ATS-safe (tables, heavy graphics, icons, unusual formats)
- Personal candidate resumes with private information
- Spam PRs

> We support real job seekers. Please help us maintain high standards.
